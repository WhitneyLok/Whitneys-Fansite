Materials

  1. Powdered sugar.
  2. Hot water.
  3. Corn syrup
  4. 2-3 Tablespoons milk
  5. Vanilla extract
  6. Bowls
  7. Creativity!
  8. Time

Steps for creating an american style sugar painting.

  1. Boil water
  2. Get a bowl
  3. Pour boiled water into bowl
  4. Pour(I've got no idea if you pour or do something else for powder things.) as much sugar(you prefer, not too much tho.) to hot water
  5. Stir the mix until its thick and grainy.
  6. Get another bowl
  7. Combine 3 cups of powder sugar, 2 tablespoons of light corn syrup in another bowl
  8. Mix it
  9. Add 2-3 tablespoons of milk,1 teaspoon of vanilla
  10. Mix them all
  11. Now you can draw what you want and add some color to it if you want
