Materials

  1. Cotton candy machine.
  2. Sticks or chopsticks.
  3. Colored granulated sugar.
  4. Creativity!
  5. Time

Steps for different shapes of cotton candy.

  1. Start the cotton candy machine.
  2. Put color granulated sugar(color of your choice) into cotton candy machine.
  3. Wait for a few moments.
  4. Put chopstick or stick over the center of cotton candy machine.
  5. Rotate the stick or chopstick as strings of sugar are comming out of the center to form layers of cotton candy around the stick/chopstick.
  6. rotate until you feel like the cotton candy on your stick/chopstick is thick enough.
  7. repeat 2-6.
  8. Use another stick/chopsticks to make the shape.
  9. You can repeat until you feel it's complete!
