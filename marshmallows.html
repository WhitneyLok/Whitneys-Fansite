Materials

  1. Gelatin
  2. Water
  3. Sugar or corn syrup
  4. Mixing bowl
  5. Mixing stick
  6. Maybe food coloring
  7. Creativity!
  8. Time!

Steps for creation of marshmallows.

  1. Mix gelatin with water
  2. Set aside.
  3. Over the stovetop, melt sugar/corn syrup with water, then heat to 240 degrees Fahrenheit.
  4. Pour sugar mixture into the bowl with gelatin and beat until the batter is thick.
  5. Pour into a pan and let sit for 6 hours.
  6. You can add food coloring if you wish to.
