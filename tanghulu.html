Materials

  1. Fruits or red and yellow hawthorn berries.
  2. Sticks/chopsticks/bamboo skewers.
  3. Water.
  4. Small Pot
  5. Creativity!
  6. Time!

Steps for creation of Bing Tang Hulu(Tanghulu).

  1. Get a stick and shove 4 fruit/berries of your choice.
  2. Use a small pot and heat about 150ml of water on medium heat.
  3. Add 200 grams of sugar gradually. Keeping most of the granules in the center of the pot.
  4. Dip the bottom of the pot into cold water, but do it slowly so you risk the crystallizing of the solution early.
  5. Then just dip the candy into the sugar solotion right away, then your done!
